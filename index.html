FAQs

Common Questions.

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us — we reply same day.

Are these sessions really free? +
Yes — completely free. No credit card, no application, no fees of any kind. Just sign up and show up. These are open virtual workshops, no strings attached.
Are these live classes or pre-recorded? +
Live. Your child joins an interactive Zoom session with a teacher and other students. They participate, ask questions, and build in real time — it's not a video to watch.
What does my child need to join? +
A laptop or desktop computer and a stable internet connection. For the Game session, a free Scratch account helps (your child can set one up in 2 minutes). For the Story session, a Gmail account. The teacher will walk through setup at the start — no prep needed.
Are these for homeschooled students too? +
Absolutely. These sessions were built with virtual and homeschool families in mind. Your child will be alongside other students who understand what it's like to learn outside a traditional classroom.
Can my child do all three? +
Yes — and we recommend it. Each session is a completely different activity, and the three workshops happen on different days of the week. You can sign up for all three in the same week of June or spread them across the month.
What grades are these for? +
Students in grades 4–8 (for the 2026–2027 school year). The sessions are designed to be accessible and engaging across that full range.
Is this related to Unbound Academy? +
Yes — these sessions give you a real taste of what Unbound afternoons look like every day. Unbound is a tuition-free virtual public charter school for Arizona grades 4–8 where students master academics in 2 hours, then spend afternoons in hands-on skill workshops like these. If you're curious about full enrollment, we're happy to chat after your session.
